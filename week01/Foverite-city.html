Kampala, Uganda

Kampala is the capital and largest city of Uganda. It is dynamic, vibrant, and built across seven historic hills, making it a unique and scenic place to explore. It serves as the national center for culture, business, and education.

The city is famous for its lively markets, rich history, and friendly people. From the historic Kasubi Tombs to the bustling streets of the central business district, Kampala blends traditional heritage with rapid modern growth perfectly.
