White Water Adventures

At White Water Adventures, our mission is to deliver unforgettable, safe, and heart-pounding river experiences for families, thrill-seekers, and nature lovers alike. Guided by our crew of certified professionals, we treat every rapid as an adventure and every guest as family.

History

Founded in 2010 by a small group of passionate river guides, White Water Adventures began with just two rafts and a deep love for the wilderness. Over the past decade, we have grown into one of the region's leading outfitters, guiding thousands of happy adventurers down some of the most beautiful and exciting rivers in the world.
